Palak Paneer

Serves: 4 people

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Spinach (Palak) Fresh or frozen, blanched and pureed.
  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) Cut into cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Onion,Tomato,Ginger,Garlic,Green chilies-Finely Chopped
  • Garam masala-A blend of ground spices,Red chili powder-For heat and color.
  • Oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Blanch the spinach
  2. Cook the spices
  3. Add tomatoes and dry spices
  4. Combine spinach puree
  5. Add paneer
