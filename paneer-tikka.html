Paneer Tikka

Serves: 4 people

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • Paneer 250-300g
  • Yogurt 1/2 cup
  • Red chili powder 1tsp
  • Turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
  • Garam Masala 1tsp
  • Oil 1tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Ginger garlic paste 1tsp

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, salt, and oil..
  2. Add the paneer cubes to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the mixture for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours) to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Preheat a grill or a grill pan. Brush it with some oil to prevent sticking
  5. Remove the paneer from the skewers or tray. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve with lemon wedges and mint chutney on the side..
