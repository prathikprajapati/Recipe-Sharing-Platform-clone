Dal Makhani

Serves: 4 people

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Whole Black Urad Dal (Sabut Urad Dal) 1 cup
  • Butter 2 tbsp
  • Kidney Beans (Rajma) 1/4 cup
  • Tomatoes, pureed 2 cups
  • Garam Masala 1 tsp
  • Ground Cumin 1 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander Powder 1 tsp
  • Red Chili Powder 1 tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Soak black lentils and rajma
  2. Precook the lentils well
  3. Butter is the key ingredient
  4. Slow cooking lentils on a lowest heat
  5. Spices are used
  6. smoking dal makhani(optional)
