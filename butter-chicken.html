Butter Chicken

Serves: 4 people

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken, diced
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 cup Cream
  • 2 cups Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in the tomato puree, cream, and spices. Stir well.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is tender and the sauce is thickened.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan.
